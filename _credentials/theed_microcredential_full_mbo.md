**Description:** # Droid Maintenance and Repair

This badge is awarded to students who have successfully completed the **Droid Maintenance and Repair course**. The course covers:
 * basics of droid maintenance
 *basics of repair 
 * how to identify and fix common problems
 * how to perform routine maintenance tasks.
